# MIP Pricing OS — Product Vision

## Mission
Build the fastest, clearest, and most premium commercial operating system for freight forwarders—from customer request through vendor buying, pricing, quote delivery, acceptance, and handoff to operations.

## Core users
- Pricing coordinators
- Sales representatives
- Operations managers
- Freight-forwarding owners
- Customers submitting and tracking requests

## Product promise
MIP Pricing OS should reduce duplicate entry, shorten quote response time, preserve commercial knowledge, and make complex multimodal requests easy to understand and execute.

## Core workflow
Customer Request → Internal Review → Vendor RFQs → Vendor Responses → Pricing → Customer Quote → Revision → Acceptance → Shipment Handoff

## Product principles
1. Enter information once and reuse it everywhere.
2. One source of truth for customers, requests, vendors, quotes, routes, and activities.
3. Every screen has one primary purpose.
4. Progressive disclosure: show only what is relevant now.
5. Mobile and desktop are both first-class experiences.
6. Internal cost and margin are always protected from customer-facing views.
7. Every important action is recorded in a timeline.
8. AI assists and recommends; users approve consequential actions.
9. Preserve history through versions and archiving rather than destructive overwrites.
10. Every new module must use the shared design system.

## Navigation philosophy
The application should feel like one continuous workspace, not unrelated pages. Requests, vendors, quotes, customers, and RFQs open in connected panels and preserve user context.

## Design direction
Premium, calm, modern, and operationally dense without feeling crowded. Inspired by high-quality productivity software and Apple interaction patterns, while remaining purpose-built for freight forwarding.

## AI roadmap
- Extract shipment details from pasted text, PDFs, screenshots, and email
- Detect missing information
- Match similar historical shipments
- Suggest vendors and previous rates
- Parse vendor replies into structured rate options
- Recommend margins and selling structures
- Prioritize follow-ups and expiring opportunities

## What we intentionally avoid
- Generic CRM complexity unrelated to freight pricing
- Duplicate customer or shipment records
- AI sending external communications without review by default
- One-off UI components that break consistency
- Feature additions that increase clicks without measurable operational value

## Long-term vision
A commercial operations platform that small and mid-sized freight forwarders can run daily, eventually integrating with warehouse, shipment, document, invoicing, and customer portal workflows.
